How It Works

Our integrated approach combines cutting-edge hardware, intelligent software, and advanced AI

01

Hardware Acquisition

Advanced hyperspectral camera system captures tissue data across multiple wavelength bands with high precision.

02

Hyperspectral Processing

Raw spectral data is processed into actionable hypercubes, revealing hidden tissue characteristics invisible to the naked eye.

03

Software Analysis

Proprietary software processes and visualizes spectral signatures for comprehensive tissue analysis.

04

AI-Driven Insights

Machine learning models with explainable AI provide clinicians with transparent, interpretable diagnostic recommendations.
